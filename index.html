Jack Thompson

International Mr Leather 2019

Jack Thompson is International Mr Leather 2019. He is also your current Leatherman of Color, The Prince of the Pridelands, The Simba Of Leather. Jack is one of the founding fathers of ONYX Northwest and current member of ONYX Mid-Atlantic. He was Mr. May 2018 for the Bare Chest Calendar. He is a drag performer and was the producer of Rebel Kings of Oakland drag troupe. He has currently relocated to Baltimore Maryland where he is now running The Rebel Kings of Baltimore troupe. Jack uses his 15 years of experience in leather and kink to give back to the community through stage managing events including Manarchy, Twisted Windows, Debauchery and the Playground stage at Folsom Street Fair. While giving back he still manages to be a consistent companion/service wolf to his husband Geoff.
